**![:pushpin:](https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/14.0/google-medium/1f4cc.png)** **Quick KPI Benchmarks (Daily / Weekly Check)**  
**Response Rate Targets**  

- SMS: **10–15%+**
- Cold Calling: **1–3%**
- Mail: **1.5–3%**

**Warm Lead![:pushpin:](https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/14.0/google-medium/1f4cc.png)s (Real Conversations)**  

- SMS: **2–4% of outreach**
- Cold Calling: **0.3–1%**
- Mail: **0.5–1.5%**

**Qualified Leads**  

- Should be **20–35%** of warm leads

**Offers Sent**  

- SMS/Mail: **7–12% of responses**
- Cold Calling: **3–7%**

**Contracts Signed**  

- SMS: **1–1.5% of outreach (strong)**
- Mail: **0.5–1%**
- Cold Calling: **0.4–0.8%**

**Contract → Close Rate**  

- Healthy: **80–92%**

**Cost Per Lead (CPL)**  

- SMS: **$15–$25**
- Cold Calling: **$10–$20**
- Mail: **$60–$120**

**Cost Per Contract (CPC)**  

- SMS: **$300–$650**
- Cold Calling: **$400–$900**
- Mail: **$1,000–$2,000**

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**  

- Target: **5×–7×**
- Under 3× = needs fixing
- Over 8× = elite
